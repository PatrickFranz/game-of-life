The Rules

There are two lifeforms in this Game Of Life simulation; wolves and rabbits. With each tick of simulation the animals have the possibility of breeding or dying.

The simulation starts with a preset number of Wolves and Rabbits being populated in a random place on the map.

  1. Any life form will die if they are alone as with under population
  2. An animal will die if it is surrounded by more than 3 others as with over population.
  3. If an animal is in close proximity to a animal of the same species there is a chance they will breed.
